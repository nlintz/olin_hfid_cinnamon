Interaction Flows

We determined the high-level interaction flows for each of our personas, based on what they expect to accomplish using our interface.

Sarah The Super Planner

Search For Courses Using Course Finding Interface

Plan Her Four Years By Adding Classes To A Future Schedule

Add Courses From Scheduler Interface

Divider

Register For Courses By Submitting A Finished Schedule

Sarah needs to create a 4-year academic plan. To assemble her plan, she searches for and selects various required and elective courses. Once she has a plan, she knows what she is going to register for, so she then registers.

Trail Blazing Tyler

Search For Courses Using Course Finding Interface

Add Courses To His Schedule From Search Interface

Register For Courses As He Finds Them Or From Schedule Interface If His Schedule Is Full

Tyler needs to find what classes he wants to take that semester. He adds classes he is interested in, then when his course schedule is satisfactory, he registers.

Francis the Follower

Search For Courses Using Course Finding Interface

Adds Classes From Search Interface

Add Courses From Scheduler Interface

Francis already knows what classes he needs to take, so he adds these classes and registers.
